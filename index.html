Director's Message

Ustad A.K. Muhammad Sakhafi Ambalakandi, a renowned Islamic scholar,Member of samast Kerala Jam-iyyathul ulama Kozhikode Jilla mushawara. General secretary of samastha Kerala jam-iyyathul ulama koduvally mekhala, lecturer and teacher from Kerala, India, is known for his expertise in Islamic theology, jurisprudence and Arabic language. He has authored several profound works on Islamic subjects and has delivered remarkable lectures at various seminars and conferences, captivating audiences with his in-depth knowledge.

He has been actively involved in Islamic education and has taught at several institutions in Kerala, nurturing students and promoting Islamic values. Respected for his dedication to scholarship and education, he continues to inspire and foster a deeper understanding of Islamic teachings.
